About Us

Welcome to Sampurna Bharat, a comprehensive Health Data Management and Information System (HDMIS) designed to transform healthcare data management in India. Our mission is to provide real-time insights to government administrators and ensure efficient healthcare services for all. Sampurna Bharat is an initiative aimed at transforming healthcare management in India through a comprehensive Health Data Management and Information System (HDMIS). Our mission is to streamline the collection, management, and analysis of health data from the grassroots level to the topmost government administrators, enabling them to make informed decisions that enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of healthcare policies.

Our Vision

To create a unified, accessible, and efficient healthcare information system that empowers patients, doctors, and government administrators, ensuring better healthcare outcomes for every citizen of India.


Our Mission

  • To develop a user-friendly platform for managing health data efficiently.
  • To provide real-time insights to government administrators for effective policy implementation.
  • To ensure data security and privacy for all users.
  • To enhance the accessibility of healthcare services through technology.

Future Plans

We aim to expand our platform to include AI-based predictive analytics, telemedicine integration, and multilingual support to cater to the diverse population of India.
